# Brote

**Category:** Coffee brewing journal
**Platform:** iOS, iPadOS
**Price:** Free
**Website:** https://brote.coffee

Brote is a specialty coffee brewing journal app for iPhone and iPad designed to help coffee enthusiasts log, track, and improve their brewing. The app focuses on recording brew parameters and maintaining a clear personal history of brews so users can refine technique and reproduce great cups.

Brote is available free on the Apple App Store.

---

## What it does

Brote lets specialty coffee enthusiasts log, track, and dial in every brew.

Users record brew parameters including:

- Dose
- Water amount
- Water temperature
- Brew time
- Grind size

Each brew can also include:

- Rating
- Tasting notes
- Photos

These logs create a personal brewing history that helps users refine recipes and repeat successful brews.

---

## Brew methods

Brote supports common specialty coffee brew methods including:

- Pour-over (V60, Hario Switch)
- AeroPress
- Espresso
- Immersion brewers
- Fully custom brew methods

Users can create their own brew methods and reuse them when logging brews.

---

## Bean management

Users can manage a collection of coffee beans with details such as:

- Roaster
- Origin
- Processing method
- Roast level
- Roast date

Beans can be archived once finished to keep the active collection organized.

---

## Equipment tracking

Equipment can optionally be linked to brew logs, including:

- Grinders
- Brewers
- Other brewing equipment

This allows users to track how different equipment affects brew results.

---

## Who it's for

Brote is designed for people who care about taste and process, including:

- Home baristas
- Specialty coffee hobbyists
- Pour-over enthusiasts
- Espresso enthusiasts
- Anyone trying to consistently reproduce a great cup

The app supports deliberate practice and recipe refinement through detailed record-keeping.

---

## What Brote is not

Brote is not:

- A coffee social network
- A marketplace for equipment
- A coffee shop finder
- A recipe database

It is a focused personal brew journal.

---

## Privacy

Brote is privacy-first.

- No account required
- No personal data collected
- All data stored locally on the user’s device
